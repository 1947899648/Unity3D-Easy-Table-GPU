=== Acknowledgments ===
Thanks to the Google Noto Fonts team for providing open-source fonts
for all the world's writing systems.

"Noto" stands for "No Tofu" — the project's mission is to eliminate
the blank boxes (tofu) that appear when a system lacks the glyphs
to display a character.

Noto Sans CJK covers Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese,
Japanese, Korean, and Latin characters. It is one of the most
comprehensive open-source multilingual fonts available today.

=== Usage ===
EasyTableGPU uses this font to provide GPU-accelerated table rendering
with full Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Latin character support.
